# Chapter 00.02 — Product Principles
**Path:** `/docs/SODA_MASTER/00.02_Product_Principles.md`
---
# Purpose
Define the permanent principles that guide every product, business, architecture and technical decision inside SODA OS.
These principles are expected to change very rarely and take precedence over implementation preferences.
---
# Product Principles
## PRINCIPLE-001 — Business First
SODA OS exists to serve real business operations before adding technical complexity or new features.
---
## PRINCIPLE-002 — No Fake Business Data
No feature should rely on fake business records once the product enters production.
Mock or seed data may exist only for development purposes and must never be presented as real business information.
---
## PRINCIPLE-003 — One Source of Truth
Every business entity must have one official owner.
Duplicate ownership of business data is not allowed.
Each major module must declare its System of Record.
---
## PRINCIPLE-004 — Production Over Prototype
Long-term production quality always takes priority over temporary demonstrations or shortcuts.
---
## PRINCIPLE-005 — English UI + Egyptian Human Layer
The product interface is written in English.
Internal discussions, documentation reviews and operational communication may be conducted in Egyptian Arabic.
---
## PRINCIPLE-006 — Simplicity Over Feature Count
Adding more features is not considered progress.
The preferred solution is the simplest one that solves the business problem correctly.
---
## PRINCIPLE-007 — Protect Long-Term Maintainability
Every implementation should remain understandable, maintainable and scalable over time.
Short-term convenience must not create long-term technical debt without clear business value.
---
## PRINCIPLE-008 — Never Redesign Stable Modules Without Clear Business Value
Stable modules should not be redesigned unless there is a verified business reason.
Visual preference alone is not a sufficient reason.
---
## PRINCIPLE-009 — Facts Before Assumptions
Documentation must distinguish between:
- FACT
- DECISION
- ASSUMPTION
- QUESTION
Assumptions must never be documented as facts.
---
## PRINCIPLE-010 — Accuracy Over Speed
An incomplete chapter is acceptable.
An inaccurate chapter is not.
When information cannot be verified, it should be marked as:
- Not yet verified
or documented as an Open Question.
---
