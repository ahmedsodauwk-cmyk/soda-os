# Purpose
Define what SODA MASTER is and establish it as the official source of truth for SODA OS.
SODA MASTER exists to ensure that every significant product, business, architecture, UX and operational decision has a permanent, searchable and maintainable record.
It evolves together with the codebase and is considered part of the product.
